About me

Spatial Data Analyst & GIS Developer

I'm a Master of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) student at the University of Minnesota and pursuing a graduate minor in computer science. I have been in the field of GIS for more than 5 years.

GIS is the scientific discipline that studies how to use computational techniques to gather, process, analyze, and visualize geographic information.

My fields of interest include web GIS and spatial data analysis.
Spatial data analysis is a special case of data analysis. My GIS background provides me a unique way to think spatially and analyze location data, which is more and more important today. Furthermore, I have a computer science undergraduate minor and have a plan to pursue a graduate minor, which gave me excellent programming skills and good knowledge about database management.
